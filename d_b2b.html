Frequently Asked Questions

How does Business Sales differ from your Design Trade Program?

Our Business Sales program is for hospitality projects, commercial retailers, multi-residential developments and more, while our Design Trade Program is for design/build industry professionals working on single-residential projects.

Why should I use Crate and Barrel Business Sales for my large-scale project?

As a member of our Business Sales program, you will receive a concierge level of service for your project that includes a dedicated associate, made to order and curated products, on-site installation and expert design services. Business Sales offers the variety and range of both our brands—Crate and Barrel and CB2—as well as customizable commercial-grade pieces to suit any project, all with our trusted quality and style.

Who qualifies to be a member of Crate and Barrel Business Sales?

The Business Sales team works with companies focused on large hospitality, commercial and residential projects. If that describes your work, simply present the following:

  1. A business card indicating your company
  2. A completed and signed application form
  3. Either: a) a copy of your business license; or b) your business tax identification number

Email business_sales@crateandbarrel.com for more application information.

Who is my Sales contact?

Once your account is established, you will have one dedicated, expert Business Sales Associate who will handle every aspect of your project. Please email business_sales@crateandbarrel.com to get started.
